# DocFlow Pack — Offer Sheet

**Product:** DocFlow Pack (SpecFlow AI P3/P4)  
**Type:** Add-on to SpecBot or DataAgent; also standalone

## One-liner

Turn agent outputs into SOPs, Confluence-ready pages, and sales-ready docs — with review discipline built in.

## Pricing

| Mode | Setup (VND) | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Attach to SpecBot / DataAgent | 10–20M | Discount vs standalone |
| Standalone | 15–25M | SOP + Confluence migration only |
| Sales team training add-on | +5–15M | Half-day workshop |

## Includes

- DocFlow skill bundle (`docflow-pack`)
- Confluence page templates (headings, tables, decision log)
- SOP authoring checklist
- 1× doc review session on sample output
- Optional: sales team training on “review before send”

## Excludes

- Full Confluence admin / space migration labor beyond 20 pages
- Legal review of content
- Ongoing doc maintenance (available as retainer)

## Deliverables

1. `docflow-pack` installed to team Cursor/Claude
2. Template pack: SOP, RFC, stakeholder update
3. Review rubric aligned to [review-rubric](https://github.com/charlieviettq/awesome-agent-skill/blob/main/docs/review-rubric.md)

## Success signal

- ≥3 SOPs published to Confluence within 30 days
- Sales team uses trace checklist on outbound RFQ responses
