# Platform Fees — SEA E-Commerce

This document covers the fee structures for major SEA marketplaces. Numbers reflect publicly available rate cards as of early 2025; always verify against each platform's current seller portal before budgeting.

---

## Fee Components (Common Across Platforms)

Every marketplace charges some combination of these:

| Component | What it is | Typical Range |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Commission** | % of GMV taken per order | 1–15% |
| **Payment processing** | Credit card / e-wallet gateway fee | 1–2% |
| **Transaction fee** | Flat per-order admin charge | $0–$0.50 |
| **Fulfillment / shipping subsidy clawback** | When platform subsidizes shipping, seller shares cost | Varies |
| **Advertising (optional)** | Sponsored listings, affiliate | Bid-based |
| **Penalty / SLA fee** | Late shipment, cancellation | $0.50–$5/order |

The total effective take rate usually lands between **8–20%** of sale price depending on category and fulfillment method.

## Shopee

Shopee is the dominant platform in Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

### Commission by Category (Malaysia / Singapore baseline — adjust for market)

| Category | Commission Rate |
|----------|----------------|
| Electronics | 1.5–3% |
| Mobile phones | 1.5% |
| Fashion & apparel | 5–8% |
| Health & beauty | 5–8% |
| Home & living | 3–5% |
| FMCG / food | 2–4% |
| Sports & outdoors | 5–7% |
| Digital goods | 3–5% |

> Commission rates vary by market. Indonesia and Vietnam rates are typically 1–2pp lower than Malaysia for the same category due to competitive pressure.

### Payment Processing Fee

- Standard: **2.12%** of order value (Malaysia)
- Vietnam: ~**1.5–2%**
- Indonesia: ~**2%**
- Philippines: ~**2%**

### Shipping Fee Structure

Shopee offers two models:

**Model A — Seller arranges shipping**
- Seller pays courier directly (J&T, SPX Express, etc.)
- No shipping subsidy exposure
- Typical: RM 5–8 first kg (Malaysia), IDR 9,000–15,000/kg (Indonesia)

**Model B — Shopee Supported Logistics (SSL)**
- Platform negotiated rates, billed to seller account
- Shopee may run free shipping campaigns — seller absorbs cost (capped at a platform-defined max per order)
- Subsidized shipping clawback: 0–RM 10 per order depending on campaign

### Worked Example: Fashion Item (Malaysia)

| Line item | Calculation | Amount (MYR) |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Sale price | — | 89.00 |
| Commission (7%) | 89 × 0.07 | 6.23 |
| Payment processing (2.12%) | 89 × 0.0212 | 1.89 |
| Shipping subsidy (capped) | — | 2.00 |
| **Total platform cost** | — | **10.12** |
| **Effective take rate** | 10.12 / 89 | **11.4%** |

### Shopee Ads Costs (Non-Mandatory)

- **Search Ads (sponsored products)**: CPC bid, minimum MYR 0.10/click; typical CPC MYR 0.30–1.50 for fashion
- **Shopee Live / affiliate**: commission payout 5–15% on top, paid to KOL/livestreamer
- Adding 10% ad spend on top of the above pushes effective take rate to ~20%

## Lazada

Lazada (Alibaba-backed) competes with Shopee across Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines. Market share is generally weaker except in parts of Thailand.

### Commission by Category

| Category | Commission Rate |
|----------|----------------|
| Electronics | 2–4% |
| Mobile phones | 2% |
| Fashion & apparel | 6–10% |
| Health & beauty | 5–8% |
| FMCG | 3–5% |
| Home & living | 4–6% |
| Toys & hobbies | 5–7% |

### Payment Processing

- **2%** flat across most markets (credit card / digital wallet)
- FPX bank transfer (Malaysia): ~1.5%

### Lazada Fulfillment (LazMall vs. 3P Seller)

**LazMall** (verified brand stores):
- Lower commission (typically 1–2pp less)
- Higher eligibility bar (trademark required)
- Mandatory SLA: ship within 24h, or MYR 5 penalty/order

**3P Standard Seller**:
- Commission as above
- Fulfillment Center (FBL — Fulfilled by Lazada): additional storage + pick-pack fee
  - Storage: ~MYR 0.07/unit/day
  - Pick & pack: MYR 1.50–3.00/order

### Worked Example: Electronics Accessory (Thailand)

| Line item | Calculation | Amount (THB) |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Sale price | — | 350.00 |
| Commission (4%) | 350 × 0.04 | 14.00 |
| Payment processing (2%) | 350 × 0.02 | 7.00 |
| FBL pick & pack | — | 35.00 |
| FBL storage (3 days avg) | 0.07 × 3 days × 1 unit | 0.21 |
| **Total platform cost** | — | **56.21** |
| **Effective take rate** | 56.21 / 350 | **16.1%** |

## Tokopedia (Indonesia Only)

Tokopedia is the largest Indonesian-origin marketplace, now merged with Gojek under GoTo Group.

### Commission

| Seller Tier | Commission |
|-------------|-----------|
| Regular seller | 1.8% |
| Power Merchant | 1.8% |
| Power Merchant Pro | 1.8% (+ subscription ~IDR 200,000/month) |
| Official Store | 2.5–3.5% (by category) |

Tokopedia's base commission is exceptionally low by SEA standards. The catch: **advertising spend is nearly mandatory** to generate traffic, especially for new stores.

### Payment Processing

- GoPay / bank transfer: **1%**
- Credit card: **2%**
- Tokopedia blends to roughly **1.5%** effective

### GoSend / Tokopedia Logistics

- No mandatory fulfillment program (unlike Shopee)
- Seller ships via GoSend, JNE, J&T, SiCepat — negotiated separately
- Typical domestic: IDR 9,000–18,000 first kg depending on route

### Advertising Reality

Because Tokopedia's algorithm heavily favors advertised listings:

- **TopAds CPC**: IDR 100–800/click for most categories
- Practical ad spend for a new store: **5–15% of GMV** to maintain visibility

Effective take rate including TopAds: **~10–18%** despite low base commission.

## TikTok Shop

TikTok Shop has aggressively expanded in Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, and Vietnam since 2022.

### Commission (as of 2024–2025)

| Market | Commission Rate |
|--------|----------------|
| Indonesia | 2–8% (category-dependent) |
| Thailand | 2–6% |
| Malaysia | 1.8–5% |
| Vietnam | 2–5% |
| Philippines | 2–5% |

TikTok Shop rates are lower than Shopee as a competitive entry tactic — expect increases.

### Affiliate / Creator Fees

This is TikTok's core differentiator. Sellers can open **Affiliate Program**:
- Creator commission: 5–20% of sale price (seller-set)
- Creators promote in videos / livestreams
- Platform takes no additional cut of affiliate commission (but base commission still applies)

Total take rate with affiliate: **7–28%** depending on creator rate set.

### Payment Processing

- ~**1–2%** depending on market; exact rates not publicly documented, assume 1.5%

### Logistics

- TikTok Fulfillment Center (TFC) available in Indonesia and Thailand
- Storage: comparable to FBA (~$0.05–0.10/unit/day)
- Pick & pack: $1–2/order

## Fee Decision Framework

### Step 1 — Calculate Unit Economics Before Choosing Platform

```
Net Revenue = Sale Price
            − COGS
            − Platform Commission
            − Payment Processing
            − Shipping Cost (to buyer or fulfillment)
            − Return Rate × (COGS + Shipping Back)
            − Allocated Ad Spend
            ─────────────────────────────────────
            = Gross Margin per Order
```

If Gross Margin per Order < 0 at target price, the platform is not viable without either raising price or cutting COGS.

### Step 2 — Factor Return Rate by Market

| Market | Typical Return Rate | Impact at 20% Return Rate (THB 500 item) |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Singapore / Malaysia | 3–5% | Low — negligible |
| Indonesia | 5–10% | Medium — ~THB 10–25 loss per unit sold |
| Vietnam | 15–20% (COD) | High — ~THB 40–60 loss per unit sold |
| Philippines | 10–15% (COD) | High — ~THB 30–50 loss per unit sold |

**COD return formula:**
```
COD Return Cost = Return Rate × (Shipping to Buyer + Return Shipping + COGS of Damaged Units)
```

For Vietnam: assume 18% return rate, THB 40 round-trip logistics = THB 7.20 per unit sold added cost.

### Step 3 — Account for Mandatory Ad Spend

| Platform & Market | Minimum Viable Ad Spend (% GMV) to Get Discovery |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Shopee (all markets) | 3–8% for new stores |
| Lazada Thailand | 3–6% |
| Tokopedia Indonesia | 5–15% |
| TikTok Shop (affiliate model) | 8–20% (creator commission) |
| TikTok Shop (own content) | 0% if video goes viral; typically 5–10% for paid |

### Step 4 — Minimum Viable Price Formula

```
MVP = COGS / (1 − commission_rate − payment_rate − shipping_rate − ad_rate − return_cost_rate − target_margin)
```

Example (Vietnam, Shopee, apparel):
- COGS: VND 80,000
- Commission: 6%
- Payment: 1.5%
- Shipping: VND 20,000 (absorbed, ~5% of target price)
- Ad spend: 5%
- Return cost: 18% returns × VND 30,000 round-trip = 5.4% of sale
- Target margin: 20%

```
MVP = 80,000 / (1 − 0.06 − 0.015 − 0.05 − 0.05 − 0.054 − 0.20)
MVP = 80,000 / 0.571
MVP ≈ VND 140,000
```

Compared to Vietnamese local competitor at VND 120,000 — this Taiwan brand is uncompetitive without either cutting COGS or accepting <20% margin.

## Hidden / Non-Obvious Fees

These are frequently missed in initial budgets:

1. **Flash sale participation fee**: Shopee / Lazada major campaigns (11.11, 12.12) require subsidized pricing. Seller may need to offer 20–30% discount while platform absorbs only a portion.

2. **Return shipping borne by seller**: For buyer-initiated returns (wrong item, change of mind), most platforms in SEA shift return shipping cost to seller for transactions under a threshold.

3. **Late shipment SLA penalties**: Failure to ship within 24–48h triggers auto-cancellation or financial penalty. Shopee: MYR 3–10/order. Lazada LazMall: stricter.

4. **Store rating impact**: Low ratings suppress organic ranking, forcing higher ad spend — an indirect cost that isn't a line item but affects effective take rate.

5. **Currency conversion**: For cross-border sales (e.g., Taiwan warehouse shipping direct to SEA buyer), platform settles in local currency. FX spread: 1–2%, plus transfer fees to repatriate funds.

6. **VAT / GST registration triggers**: Once exceeding market-specific revenue thresholds, seller must register for local tax:
   - Thailand: THB 1.8M/year (~USD 50K)
   - Malaysia: MYR 500K/year (~USD 110K)
   - Indonesia: IDR 4.8B/year (~USD 300K)
   - Vietnam: VND 100M/year (~USD 4K) — very low threshold

## Shopee vs Lazada vs TikTok — Quick Selection Logic

```
IF entering Indonesia:
    → Start Tokopedia + Shopee dual-listing (different customer bases)
    → TikTok Shop if product is visual/impulse

IF entering Vietnam:
    → Shopee first (dominant); price for 18% COD returns
    → TikTok Shop for Gen Z fashion/beauty

IF entering Thailand:
    → Shopee or Lazada depending on category (check current market share)
    → Social commerce (LINE, TikTok) mandatory supplement

IF entering Malaysia:
    → Shopee dominant; Lazada for electronics
    → Lowest complexity market for first SEA entry

IF entering Philippines:
    → Shopee dominant; COD returns a major budget line
    → Island logistics add 20–40% to shipping vs mainland markets

IF entering Singapore:
    → Shopee/Lazada/Amazon all viable
    → Credit card dominant; no COD headache
    → Tiny market (6M) — use as brand proof-of-concept before scaling
```

## Data Sources and Update Cadence

Platform fee schedules change frequently (often annually around Q4). The figures above are directionally accurate but must be verified:

- **Shopee**: seller.shopee.com.my / .vn / .co.id → "Fees & Charges" section
- **Lazada**: seller.lazada.com → Seller Center → Commission Rate
- **Tokopedia**: seller.tokopedia.com → Official Store / Power Merchant documentation
- **TikTok Shop**: seller-{market}.tiktok.com → Commission Policy

Check before each fiscal year budget and before committing to a major campaign, as platform-initiated changes are common without advance notice.
