# Financial News Sources & Beats

Financial journalism in Taiwan and globally relies on tiered source ecosystems. This guide lists official repositories, regulator announcements, research firms, and market-data providers for each beat.

## Tier 1: Official Disclosures (highest credibility)

### Taiwan Market

- **公開資訊觀測站** (MOPS — Market Observation Post System)
  - URL: https://mops.twse.com.tw
  - What it is: Official repository for listed companies' filings (年報、季報、重大訊息、董事會決議)
  - Use: Base source for earnings, dividend announcements, regulatory filings
  - Timestamp: Publication time determines if material-non-public-information rules apply
  - Beat coverage: All sub-types (earnings, IPO, M&A, regulatory)

- **臺灣證券交易所 (TWSE) — 新聞公告區**
  - URL: https://www.twse.com.tw
  - What it is: Official TWSE announcements (上市公司公告、指數規則變更、停牌公告)
  - Use: Market structure changes, index rebalancing, trading halts
  - Beat coverage: Market data, regulatory events

- **財務委員會** (FSC — Financial Supervisory Commission)
  - URL: https://www.fsc.gov.tw
  - What it is: Regulator announcements (監管令、政策通知、罰款公告)
  - Use: Regulatory interpretation, compliance changes, enforcement actions
  - Beat coverage: Regulatory-policy beat

- **中央銀行** (Central Bank of Taiwan)
  - URL: https://www.cbc.gov.tw
  - What it is: Monetary-policy decisions (利率決議、貨幣供給、匯率政策、公開市場操作)
  - Use: Macro-economic news, interest-rate impact on earnings
  - Beat coverage: Industry trends (if relevant to borrowing costs)

- **主計總處** (Directorate-General of Budget, Accounting and Statistics)
  - URL: https://www.dgbas.gov.tw
  - What it is: Official national statistics (GDP, CPI, labor force, inflation)
  - Use: Economic context for earnings analysis, sector comparisons
  - Beat coverage: Industry trends, macro backdrop

- **櫃買中心** (Taiwan OTC Exchange)
  - URL: https://www.tpex.org.tw
  - What it is: OTC-listed companies' filings (emerging-growth companies)
  - Use: SME financial news, IPO pipeline pre-listing

### International

- **SEC EDGAR** (US)
  - URL: https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar
  - What it is: 10-K (annual), 10-Q (quarterly), 8-K (current events) filings by US-listed companies
  - Use: US company financial news, cross-border M&A involving US firms

- **HKEX** (Hong Kong)
  - URL: https://www.hkexnews.hk
  - What it is: Hong Kong Exchange announcements and listed-company filings
  - Use: Hong Kong-listed companies, Greater China financial news

## Tier 2: Professional Research & Market Data

### Analyst Consensus & Equity Research

- **Bloomberg Terminal** (subscription)
  - Consensus earnings estimates, analyst ratings, multiples by peer
  - Standard in institutional newsrooms; individual reporters use public summaries

- **Refinitiv Eikon** (subscription)
  - Similar to Bloomberg; good for fixed-income research and commodities

- **I/B/E/S** (Institutional Brokers' Estimate System)
  - Consensus EPS, revenue estimates from sell-side analysts
  - Often accessible through Bloomberg

- **Morningstar** (partial free, premium paid)
  - Analyst reports, fund/equity research summaries
  - More retail-focused; good for SME-scale companies

### Taiwan-Specific Research Providers

- **台灣股市資訊網** (Taiwan Stock Information) — free consensus estimates
- **XQ 全球贏家** (XQ Global Investors) — retail-friendly data platform
- **鉅亨網** (Cnyes) — financial news aggregator with some original research

## Tier 3: Company Communications (secondary, requires credibility check)

- **Investor Relations (IR) releases** — company press releases, investor updates
  - Use with caution: date may differ from official MOPS filing
  - Always cross-check with 公開資訊觀測站 timestamp
  - Disclosure: mark as "company stated" / "company guidance", not fact

- **Earnings call transcripts** — CFO/CEO commentary, analyst Q&A
  - Primary source for management intent; not official filing
  - Mark quotes as "in earnings call", with date and source (company IR, FactSet, etc.)

- **Investor presentations** — pitch decks, investor days
  - Forward-looking; use "company projects" / "management guidance" language
  - Requires cautious framing (not audited)

---

## Tier 4: Secondary & Opinion Sources (use with skepticism)

- **市場人士說法** (unnamed "market sources", "banking sources", "industry watchers")
  - Red flag: no transparency on stake or expertise
  - Use only if corroborated by Tier 1-2 source
  - Require editor sign-off + real risk to source for anonymity

- **新聞轉述** (other media reporting on the company)
  - Go to primary source (Tier 1-3) instead
  - If using news relay, cite original source ("per Reuters report citing company statement")

## Beat-Specific Source Strategy

### Earnings Reporting
1. Check 公開資訊觀測站 for official announcement (time-stamp)
2. Pull audited financial statements from year/quarterly report
3. Get earnings call transcript for CFO commentary
4. Cross-check analyst estimates (Bloomberg consensus) for context
5. Avoid: company press release as primary source; use as supplement only

### IPO / M&A
1. 公開資訊觀測站 重大訊息 for official deal announcement
2. 公司簽呈 (prospectus for IPO) for valuation & terms
3. SEC EDGAR 或 公開資訊觀測站 for deal structure documents
4. Financial advisors' statements (but disclose advisor relationships)
5. Regulators (FSC for approval status)

### Regulatory / Policy
1. FSC / 金管會 official announcement
2. Regulatory text / 公告法規
3. Industry body reactions (工業總會, 金融總會, etc.) — mark as stakeholder stance
4. Avoid: lobbyist commentary without attribution

### Industry Trends
1. 公開資訊觀測站 for sector-specific companies' earnings
2. Research reports (analyst consensus on sector)
3. 主計總處 statistics for market-size / growth context
4. Caution: avoid "industry watchers" without institutional affiliation

---

## Credibility Red Flags

- **Unnamed sources on financial data** — all numbers must trace to official filings or named analyst/research firm
- **"Market consensus" without citation** — always specify which Bloomberg/Refinitiv estimate; consensus shifts
- **Press release as sole source** — always check 公開資訊觀測站 or official filing for the real announcement
- **Analyst downgrades without stake disclosure** — if analyst's firm has underwriting relationship or holds stock, disclose it
- **"Industry experts" with commercial interest** — vendors, consultants, lobbies have axes to grind; disclose affiliation
